About Me

Dedicated to engineering digital spaces where aesthetic luxury and pure accessibility converge.

I am a front-end accessibility specialist with a strong passion for making the web accessible to everyone, including users with visual, auditory, motor, or cognitive impairments. I approach web design not just as a developer, but as a digital advocate, ensuring all visual beauty is represented in semantic structures.

With expertise in screen reader optimization, custom keyboard navigation design, and high-performance frontend engineering, I build projects that deliver incredible, compliant experiences under the hood.

Core Competencies

  • Accessible Rich Internet Applications (WAI-ARIA)
  • Semantic HTML5 Structure
  • WCAG 2.1 AA/AAA Guidelines
  • Keyboard-Navigable Controls
  • Lighthouse SEO & Performance Auditing
  • Responsive CSS Grid & Flexbox
  • Screen Reader Testing (NVDA, VoiceOver)
